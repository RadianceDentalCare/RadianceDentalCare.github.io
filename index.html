Importance of Child Oral Health

Tooth problems are common in children. Careful attention and care must be taken to their oral hygiene in order to prevent cavities, dental infections, and oral disease.

At Radiance Dental Care, we provide preventive and restorative care for all types of dental problems in children with special expertise in dental fillings, sealants, fluoride treatments, tooth extractions, root canals, and pediatric surgeries of soft tissues in the oral cavity.

We also offer generalized treatments for children who suffer from dental pain, trauma, and infections. Abnormalities like cleft lips and palates, decayed teeth, malocclusion, and TMJ dysfunctions can all be treated on time and cured to normalcy.
